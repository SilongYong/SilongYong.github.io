Silong Yong

I am a master student in Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University advised by Prof. Katia Sycara. I got my Bachelor's degree in Automation from Tsinghua University in 2023.

My research interests lie in 3D computer vision, robotics and graphics. Specifically, I am interested in designing algorithms that model the 3D world in a more natural and sophisticated way, benefitting downstream usages such as robotics and graphics.
