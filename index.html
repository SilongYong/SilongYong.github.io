Silong Yong(雍子隆)

I am a master student in Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University advised by Prof. Katia Sycara. I got my Bachelor's degree in Automation from Tsinghua University in 2023 under the supervision of Prof. Song-Chun Zhu. During my undergrad study, I had a wonderful journey at Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence(BIGAI), supervised by Dr. Xiaojian Ma and Dr. Siyuan Huang.

My research interests lie in computer vision, robotics, learning representation and reasoning. Specifically, I am interested in designing algorithms that model the 3D world in a more natural and sophisticated way, benefitting downstream usages such as robotics and graphics.
